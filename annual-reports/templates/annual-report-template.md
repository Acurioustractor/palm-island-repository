*[Traditional acknowledgment in local language and English]*

We acknowledge that this report covers activities on the traditional lands of [Traditional Owner Group]. We pay our respects to elders past, present, and emerging, and acknowledge their continuing connection to land, sea, and community.

### Cultural Protocols
This report has been prepared following strict cultural protocols:
- All elder stories included with explicit permission
- Community stories reviewed and approved by storytellers
- Photos and media used with full consent of all individuals
- Cultural advisors consulted throughout the preparation process
- Sensitive cultural information appropriately protected

### Data Sovereignty Statement
This report represents an exercise in Indigenous data sovereignty. All data, stories, and images contained within remain the intellectual property of the Palm Island community. We control how our data is collected, stored, and used, ensuring it serves our community's priorities and values.

### Use and Sharing Guidelines
- **Community Use:** This report is freely available to all Palm Island community members

- **Commercial Use:** Commercial use of any content is prohibited without explicit community consent
