## Executive Summary

This roadmap synthesizes recommendations from comprehensive platform review into a **pragmatic, prioritized implementation plan** that balances:
- **Quick wins** (immediate impact with minimal effort)
- **Foundation work** (essential infrastructure for future growth)
- **Strategic initiatives** (transformative, high-value features)
- **Resource constraints** (realistic staffing and budget)

**Timeline:** 24 months (2 years)
**Investment:** $130,000-$180,000 total
**Expected ROI:** $150,000+ in cost savings + transformative community impact

### 1.2 Priority Definitions

**P1 - Critical (Do First):**
- High impact, low-medium effort
- Foundation for other features
- Immediate user value
- Quick wins for momentum

**P2 - High (Plan & Do):**
- High impact, high effort
- Strategic importance
- Requires planning and resources
- Transformative value

**P3 - Medium (Do Later):**
- Medium impact, low-medium effort
- Enhances existing features
- Can wait until P1/P2 complete
- Nice-to-have improvements

**P4 - Low (Defer):**
- Low-medium impact, high effort
- Future enhancements
- Requires P1/P2 foundation
- Revisit in Year 3+
