## üî• Cultural Protocols - Keep It Safe & Respectful

### Before Sharing Any Story:
1. **Do I have permission?** ‚úÖ Make sure everyone mentioned is okay with sharing
2. **Is this appropriate to share?** ‚úÖ Check if it's private/sacred/sensitive  
3. **Who should be credited?** ‚úÖ Give proper credit to storytellers/photographers
4. **What sharing level?** ‚úÖ Public, Community-Only, or Restricted access

### For Elder Stories - Extra Care:
- **Always** get explicit permission from the elder or their family
- **Never** assume traditional knowledge can be shared publicly
- **Always** have a cultural advisor review the story first
- **Remember** that some stories are for certain people/times/places only

### Red Flags - When NOT to Share:
‚ùå Sacred or ceremonial information  
‚ùå Private family matters  
‚ùå Stories told in confidence  
‚ùå Anything that could harm someone  
‚ùå Photos from funerals or sorry business  

**When in doubt, ask a cultural advisor!**

## üìä How Annual Reports Work

### The Magic: Your Stories Become Our Report!
Every story you share throughout the year automatically becomes part of our annual report. No external consultants needed - it's all from our community, by our community.

### Timeline Each Year:
- **January-October:** Community members share stories all year
- **November:** Review stories and choose highlights for report  
- **December:** Community reviews and approves draft report
- **January:** New report released to celebrate the previous year

### Your Role:
- **Share stories** throughout the year
- **Suggest highlights** when asked
- **Review drafts** when shared
- **Celebrate** when the report is released!

## üåä Common Questions

### "I'm not good with computers - can I still contribute?"
**Yes!** You can:
- Write your story on paper and someone can type it up
- Tell your story to someone who can write it down
- Ask family members to help you use the templates
- Come to community sessions where people help each other

### "What if I make a mistake in the template?"
**No problem!** Community volunteers will help fix:
- Spelling and grammar
- Technical formatting
- File organization
- Getting photos uploaded

### "Can I change my mind about sharing a story?"
**Absolutely!** You can:
- Remove stories you no longer want to share
- Change the sharing level (public to community-only, etc.)
- Update information if things change
- Add more details later

### "How do I know if my story is culturally appropriate?"
**Great question!** You can:
- Check the cultural guidelines in this folder
- Ask a cultural advisor for guidance
- Start with community-only sharing level if unsure
- Talk to elders if it involves traditional knowledge

### "What happens to stories after I share them?"
Your stories:
- Stay in our secure community repository
- May be included in annual reports (with your permission)
- Help preserve our community history
- Remain under community control always
- Can be removed if you change your mind
